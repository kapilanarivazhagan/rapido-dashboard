# Rapido Operations Analytics Dashboard

A business intelligence dashboard designed to monitor operational KPIs, mobility performance metrics, reporting workflows, and business insights.

---

## Overview

This project focuses on operational analytics and KPI monitoring for mobility and fleet operations.

The dashboard helps track operational efficiency, business performance, servicing metrics, and reporting insights to support data-driven operational decisions.

---

## Key Features

- KPI performance tracking
- Operational analytics dashboards
- Business reporting workflows
- Trend analysis & monitoring
- Data visualization & insights
- Performance reporting
- Operational intelligence support
